25 Years of Scientific Publishing

An editorial timeline shaped by continuity and development

The journal’s twenty-five-year history reflects the gradual consolidation of an editorial mission attentive to biological products, evolving scientific priorities, and the demands of trustworthy publication practice.

Foundation

The beginning of a specialist publishing trajectory

The journal was established as a dedicated publication space for questions surrounding biological products, with an emphasis on scientific rigor and relevance to prevention, diagnosis, and treatment.

Editorial evolution

A platform connecting research, regulation, and practice

Across its development, the journal broadened the dialogue between laboratory science, quality control, standardization, regulatory procedures, and clinical application, reflecting the interdisciplinary nature of the field.

Open access commitment

Accessible scholarship without financial barriers

The journal’s Diamond Open Access model, together with CC BY 4.0 licensing, reinforces an editorial commitment to openness for both readers and authors while supporting broader circulation of scientific knowledge.

Editorial standards

Publication practice grounded in established guidance

Peer review and editorial decision-making are informed by COPE, WAME, and ICMJE principles, reflecting a sustained emphasis on research integrity, editorial independence, and responsible scholarly communication.

2025

International visibility in a new stage of development

The journal’s inclusion in Scopus Accepted Titles May 2025, alongside its presence in the Russian Science Citation Index and DOAJ, marks an important stage in the wider visibility of its scientific profile.

Indexing, Open Access, and Editorial Standards

Trust built through openness, structure, and editorial responsibility

The journal’s scholarly presence is supported not by promotional claims, but by publication model, editorial guidance, indexing status, and a stable commitment to accessible scientific communication.

Diamond Open Access Free for both readers and authors, with publication access aligned to openness rather than fees.
CC BY 4.0 Articles are published under a license that supports lawful reuse with appropriate attribution.
Editorial guidance Publication practice follows COPE, WAME, and ICMJE principles for responsible editorial work.
Submission languages Manuscripts may be submitted in Russian or English, with manuscripts expected in British English.

Indexed and discoverable

The journal’s visibility is strengthened by inclusion in Scopus Accepted Titles May 2025, the Russian Science Citation Index, and DOAJ. It also maintains a publication environment attentive to open citation and open research initiatives.

These elements do not replace scholarly quality; rather, they provide part of the framework through which research becomes more consistently discoverable, traceable, and usable within academic communication.

Journal Today

Continuity of mission in a changing scientific landscape

The present-day journal continues to publish work across research, review, methodology, and commentary formats, preserving thematic continuity while engaging with contemporary biomedical priorities.

What the journal publishes

  • Reviews, including narrative reviews and systematic reviews with meta-analysis
  • Original research and controlled clinical trials
  • Case reports and scientific methodology articles
  • Editorials, commentaries, and responses to published discussion
  • Manuscripts submitted in Russian or English within a specialist scholarly framework

Why the journal remains relevant

Biological products continue to demand careful attention to research quality, product characterization, laboratory evaluation, regulatory reasoning, and clinical application. The journal remains relevant because it publishes at those intersections rather than within only one disciplinary silo.

Its continued value lies in bringing together scientific evidence, methodological reflection, and publication formats that remain meaningful for researchers, expert institutions, and professional audiences working in adjacent domains.
